Ethereum
Ethereum is a virtual machine built on top of the blockchain that lets you create and run smart contracts that then run on the blockchain.

We share music, art, poetry, equity in companies, ideas, code, etc. What Ethereum does is take the idea of "Digital trustless money transfer" and expands it to "Digital trustless value transfer"â€‹
